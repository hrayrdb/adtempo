Public Relations (PR) Officer

About the Role:
We're on the lookout for a dedicated PR Officer with a strong sense of initiative and a passion for building lasting relationships. This role will play a vital part in enhancing our brand presence and ensuring transparent, impactful communication with both our clients and the public. If you're proactive, organized, and driven by creative problem-solving, we'd love to hear from you.

What You’ll Do:

  • Work closely with various departments, ensuring smooth information flow and alignment with management on PR strategies.
  • Plan and coordinate PR events, showcasing our brand values and representing the company as a key spokesperson.
  • Identify and pursue collaboration opportunities, including sponsorships, partnerships, and advertising to expand our reach.
  • Serve as the primary contact for media and other inquiries, managing responses and building a positive public image.
  • Craft comprehensive PR reports that capture our efforts, successes, and areas for improvement.
  • Lead resolution efforts on any PR issues that arise, mitigating risks to our brand reputation.
  • Oversee multimedia projects, including photography and videography, that support our promotional and branding initiatives.

What We’re Looking For:

  • Previous experience in a PR or related role, with a proven ability to drive results in a fast-paced environment.
  • Solid proficiency in MS Office and various social media platforms.
  • Experience or familiarity with project management processes to streamline PR activities and campaigns.
  • Exceptional communication skills, both verbal and written, allowing you to connect effectively with diverse audiences.
  • Strong organizational skills, attention to detail, and the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously.
  • A creative thinker with a problem-solving mindset and a readiness to innovate in all PR-related activities.

Type: Freelance, with potential for a full-time role offering a salary plus performance-based incentives.
